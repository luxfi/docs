## Intra-Lux Transfers (Warp)

Transfers between Lux C-Chain and sovereign L1 chains use the native Warp messaging protocol. These are fast (sub-second) and do not require MPC signing.

## External Chain Transfers (MPC)

Transfers to and from external chains (Ethereum, Bitcoin) use MPC threshold signing. The bridge locks assets on the source chain and mints wrapped tokens on the destination.
