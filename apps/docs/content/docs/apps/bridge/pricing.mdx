The Lux Bridge charges per-transaction fees. No monthly subscription required.

## Transfer Fees

| Route | Fee | Min | Max |
|-------|-----|-----|-----|
| Lux C ↔ Subnet | 0.1% | $0.01 | $50 |
| Lux ↔ Ethereum | 0.2% + gas | $1.00 | $100 |
| Lux ↔ Bitcoin | 0.3% + network fee | $5.00 | $200 |

## Gas Costs

Gas fees on the destination chain are paid by the user. The bridge relays the transaction and the MPC nodes sign it.
