## K8s Operator

The Kubernetes operator is free for all plans. CRDs sync secrets from KMS to your cluster.

## Self-Hosted

Self-hosted deployments are available on the Enterprise plan. Includes:
- Docker/K8s deployment
- Air-gapped support
- Custom HSM backends
- Unlimited secrets and users
