## Architecture

Base (2022) is the data plane — encrypted vault, CRDT sync, realtime APIs. Lux (2020) is the trust kernel — 15 chains on every validator. Lattice (2019) is the crypto — FHE from scratch, GPU-accelerated.

Three tiers: AES-256-GCM + ML-KEM (default), TFHE threshold reveal (high-value), CKKS confidential compute (new products).
